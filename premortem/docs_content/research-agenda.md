# Research Agenda

The research agenda identifies important assumptions in the causal graph that
can be tested before a go/no-go decision.

Each research item should state:

- What the pre-mortem assumes.
- Why the assumption is uncertain.
- How to resolve it.
- Who or what to study.
- What finding would change the decision.

Good research items include concrete methods, sample populations, thresholds,
and decision rules.

Typical command:

`premortem job generate research-agenda`
