# Personas

Personas should be specific stakeholder perspectives, not generic roles.

A good panel includes:

- An internal champion.
- A skeptic or person with conflicting incentives.
- Someone directly affected by the initiative.
- An external partner, customer, regulator, or evaluator.
- A spread across hierarchy: frontline, middle management, and leadership.

Quality markers:

- Specific institutional position.
- Concrete incentives and constraints.
- Distinct concerns from the other personas.
- Enough detail that their failure narratives will diverge.

Typical command:

`premortem job generate personas --context "..." --requirements "..."`
